Welcome to Jarvis Ontario, located at the crossroads of highways 3 & 6, and home of the JarvisFest.

This summer festival is an exciting and vibrant part of our community. JarvisFest is an excellent way to reconnect, socialize, and enjoy the hospitality of our little town.

We offer a variety of entertainment options that are suitable for all ages. From live music, to the kid's midway, a disc golf tournament, and fireworks, there is something for everyone here.

The relaxed and friendly atmosphere of JarvisFest makes it a great place to  make memories.

Come visit us - you won't be disappointed! 


What's To Come in 2024
