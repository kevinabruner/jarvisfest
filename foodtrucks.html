JOIN US AS A FEATURED FOOD TRUCK AT JARVISFEST!


Are you a culinary entrepreneur with a passion for serving up delicious eats on wheels? We're on the lookout for top-notch food trucks to be a highlight of the upcoming third season of JarvisFest

We are excited to invite local food trucks to be a part of the culinary delight at JarvisFest. If you have a mouthwatering menu, we want you to roll in and showcase your creations. We're seeking a diverse range of food trucks offering a variety of food to tantalize the tase buds of our festival guests. From savory street food classics to indulgent sweet treats, we welcome all types of cuisine.


Event: JarvisFest

Date: August 16, 17, and 18 2024

Location: Jarvis Lions Park


What JarvisFest is looking For:

  • Burgers and Sandwiches
  • Pizza
  • Asian Fusion
  • International Flavors
  • Vegan and Vegetarian Options
  • Desserts and Sweet Treats

And More!


Benefits:

  • Prime location within the festival grounds
  • Opportunity to showcase your culinary creations and build brand awareness
  • Opportunity to maximize sales and grow your customer base

Requirements:

  • Valid food truck license and health permits
  • Compliance with local health and safety regulations
  • Availability to participate for the entire duration of the festival

Join us in serving up a feast of flavors and fun at JarvisFst!


To apply for a spot as a food truck vendor, please fill out the Food Vendor Application form.
