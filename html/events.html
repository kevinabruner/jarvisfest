What's new this year

Thanks to our sponsors and you, our community, JarvisFest is growing! This year we are thrilled to welcome Townsend Amusements who will bring an assortment of rides and games for all ages to enjoy. On top of that, we're proud to be going local for the beer tent this year with businesses such as Meuse Brewing, and Hounds of Erie Winery. Another exciting event is the Smash Room & Car Smash by ABEL Enterprises who is celebrating 40 years as a charity and social enterprise in Norfolk County.
