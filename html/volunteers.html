VOLUNTEER AT JARVISFEST!


Are you ready to dive into the excitement of the vibrant JarvisFest atmosphere? Do you thrive in dynamic environments filled with music, art, and fun? Then we want YOU to be a part of our volunteer team for our third season of JarvisFest!


We're on the lookout for positive, upbeat, and friendly individuals who are eager to contribute to the success of our festival, celebrating our community. As a volunteer, you'll assist us in various roles and aid in the smooth operation of our events.


Position: JarvisFest Volunteer

Date: August 16, 17, and 18 2024

Location: Jarvis Lions Park


Volunteer Opportunities:

  • Welcoming and directing attendees at the gate
  • Taking donations
  • Handing out festival items/givaways

Benefits:

  • Gain hands-on experience
  • Connect with fellow volunteers and festival-goers
  • Students gain needed volunteer hours

Requirements:

  • Friendly and approachable demeanor
  • Ability to work well in a team
  • Availability to commit to at least one shift during JarvisFest

Join us in celebrating music, art, food, and community spirit at JarvisFest!


To sign up to be a JarvisFest volunteer, please fill out the JarvisFest Volunteer form.
