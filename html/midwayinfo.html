Midway - Townsend Amusements


JarvisFest has partnered with Townsend Amusements for this years midway entertainment. Towsend Amusements is a well-established name in the world of carnivals and fairs. With decades of experience, the company has built a reputation for creating a family-friendly environment with a diverse array of attractions.


Some of the rides include:

  • Chilly Willy
  • Fun Slide
  • Pyramid Bounce
  • Swings
  • El Paso Train Ride
  • Rocket Race
  • Tubs of Fun
  • Twizzler
  • Wacky Shack

And more!
