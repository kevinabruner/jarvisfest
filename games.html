Join in the JarvisFest fun 


Join us for a thrilling day of outdoor fun at JarvisFest. Grab your friends and get ready to test your skills

in the slo-pitch and disc golf tournaments, where friendly competition and exciting moments await.

Don't worry if you're new to the sports; we welcome players of all levels!


JarvisFest will also have a car show this year, which will feature and impressive array of vehicles.


So mark your calendars, bring your A-game, and prepare to be entertained.

We can't wait to see you there!
